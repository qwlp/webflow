Challenge InstructionsExplore Webflow on your own, creating a new page for the Detox spa package.See the solution.jpg image for how the page should look on your desktop.Suggested approach:1. Take one of the unused pages in the site and repurpose it as the Detox page by changing its title and URL and removing all existing content.2. Add a header and footer.3. Copy over the opening graphic and text from the Home or Spa pages. Repurpose it with the spa image and text in the exercise files folder.4. Set up a new section of the site after the opening graphic with details about the service. Explore the rich text block to display the text on the left side.On the right side, make a pale indigo box with 1rem of padding on all four sides. Separate each line of text by 1rem of space.5. Check the page and adjust for smaller breakpoints.6. Go back to the Spa page and the Home page and link the buttons for the Detox service to this new Detox page.
